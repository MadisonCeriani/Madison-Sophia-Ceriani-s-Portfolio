Tableau Dashboard

A data storytelling dashboard that combines map, chart visualizations, and contextual narrative to reveal trends in an accessible format.This project is the final for a Data Visualization Tools & Techniques class. The steps were completed over several weeks. The first objective was to choose data that is credible. Artifact 03

Book Cover Design

A book cover concept for Stephen King's Pet Sematary, balancing typography, mood, and composition to reflect genre expectations and audience tone. This project was completed using Adobe Illustrator. The objective was to rely on typography and minimal visual elements to create an effective book cover. The design features red lines that resemble cat scratches; this is meant to foreshadow the dark turn of events that befall the main character’s family after the death of their cat. Children play a major role in the unfolding of the events of the story, and this is reflected in the font chosen for the title which resembles a child’s writing.

Artifact 04

Usability Study Poster

A research poster for ASU's student showcase that visualizes study method, findings, and user-experience recommendations in a clear presentation format.This project involved utilizing design principles to communicate the findings of the usability study effectively. Color is an important element of design. As this poster demonstates the strategic use of ASU's colors to communicate the key messages, it demonstrates how color can be used to enhance the visual appeal and readability of the data. Research is another important aspect of this project. Usability study are not just for web design, a usability study can provide useful data for effective design of various things. In this project, the effectiveness of the design of a bookstore is evaluated, and through the study it was determined that the business could retain more customers through more effective signage.
