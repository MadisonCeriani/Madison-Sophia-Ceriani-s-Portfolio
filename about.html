About Me

My Story

Hi, my name is Madison Ceriani. I have lived in Arizona most of my life, but I am originally from Pennsylvania. In my spare time, I love to read. My favorite book is Coraline, and fantasy is my favorite genre. I am a Technical Communication graduate from Arizona State University with a strong interest in professional writing, user experience, and digital design. I enjoy being creative, whether that's by drawing, crocheting, or creating content that combines effective communication with thoughtful design to make complex information easier for audiences to understand.

My experience includes web development internship work, design with Adobe tools and Canva, and writing projects with clear objectives for defined audiences. Across those projects, I developed skills in critical thinking, research, editing, time management, collaboration, and attention to detail. I am excited to continue growing as a professional. I currently work as a server and as an associate alumni advisor at Podium, where I have gained valuable experience in teamwork, working in fast-paced environments, effective communication, and client satisfaction.
