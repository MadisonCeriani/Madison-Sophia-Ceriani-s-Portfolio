Writing Artifacts

Five writing artifacts with explanatory paragraphs and embedded PDF viewing space for each project.

Artifact 01

Poppin Joe's Style Guide

This style guide establishes writing and visual standards tailored to Poppin Joe's brand voice. It focuses on consistency across headings, image treatments, and copy choices so future content remains cohesive and recognizable.

Artifact 02

Freelance Comprehensive Editing Project

This project involved writing for a client audience outside the classroom. The objective was to edit a travel guide, verify claims, and improve readability through detailed research and revision.

Artifact 03

Grant Proposal

This proposal was drafted to support an Arizona Welfare League application for a mini-grant opportunity. It demonstrates persuasive writing, audience awareness, and structured evidence to support funding impact.

Artifact 04

Personas and Storytelling

This project develops a complete persona and associated narrative to model user-centered communication. It combines research framing with storytelling techniques to make audience motivations clear and actionable.

Artifact 05

Report on Intellectual Property

This report outlines key distinctions in intellectual property and copyright coverage, including practical examples. It was designed to make legal concepts more approachable for creators, students, and small organizations.
